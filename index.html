About This Report

The digital landscape has fundamentally reshaped how individuals seek local services, with an increasing reliance on "near me" and "in my location" searches. This pervasive search behavior underscores a clear user intent for immediate, geographically relevant discovery. However, a comprehensive analysis reveals that while general online directories exist, they frequently fall short in capturing the granular specificity required for niche services. This deficiency creates significant gaps for targeted online platforms that can precisely match specialized demand with often fragmented supply.

This interactive application is designed to bridge that gap. By visualizing the potential of various underserved niches in Lichfield, it aims to provide entrepreneurs, community leaders, and investors with a clear, data-driven tool to identify and act upon local opportunities. The goal is to foster a more connected, efficient, and vibrant local ecosystem by making hidden gems discoverable and supporting the growth of specialized local businesses and services.
